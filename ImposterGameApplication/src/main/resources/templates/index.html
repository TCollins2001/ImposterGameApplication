T.C Imposter Game is a multiplayer social deduction game where one player is kept in the dark.
Join a room, receive a role, and try to uncover the imposter — or survive as one...

How to Play!
  • One player is secretly the imposter
  • Everyone else receives the same category and question
  • The imposter does not see the question and will have to answer based on the category alone
  • Answer carefully and try to guess who's the imposter
  • After everyone votes, the imposter reveals themselves
  • If more than half guessed correctly, the group wins. Otherwise, the imposter wins
